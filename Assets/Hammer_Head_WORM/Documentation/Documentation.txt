	
--------------Hammer head Worm(Bipalium)low poly----------------------------

Bipalium is a genus of large predatory land planarians.They are often loosely called
"Hammerhead worms", "Broadhead planarians" or "Showel headed garden worms" because of the distinctive shape of their head region.

About pack:
This pack contains 3d Model and Animation's of "Hammer head worm".You can use this package in your game.

Technical Details:-
				
		- Vertex count : 770
		- Tris count : 1536
		- Animations : 9 (Idle-1, Walk-1, Walk-2, Run-1, Run-2, Tail animation, Fly, Attack, Anim_01)
		- Texture Variations : 8
		- Used Material(s) : Unity Standard Shader
		- Texture maps : Albedo/Base Color
		- Texture Size : 2048(.png)
		- Prefabs : 16

Other features:-
	
	- Smooth, Unique, Realistic animations
	- Included static, non-static models
	- Simple colliders attached
	- Easy to change colors
	- Mobile, AR supported
